Frequently Asked Questions

Is GovtExamPath really free? ⌄
Yes, 100% free! We don't charge for any guidance, tools or resources on this website. We are funded by ads, which help us keep the site running for free.
Which exam should I give after BTech? ⌄
BTech graduates can apply for GATE (for PSU jobs like ONGC, BHEL, NTPC), UPSC IES (Engineering Services), UPSC CSE (IAS), SSC CGL, and many State PSC exams. Use our eligibility checker on the homepage to see all options for your specific branch!
Do you provide coaching or study material? ⌄
We don't provide coaching. We guide you to the best FREE resources available online — YouTube channels, government websites, free PDFs and test series. We believe you don't need paid coaching to crack government exams!
Can I suggest a new exam to add to the website? ⌄
Absolutely! Use the contact form above or DM us on Instagram @govtexampath. We're constantly adding new exam pages based on user requests.
How accurate is the eligibility checker? ⌄
Our eligibility checker is based on official notifications from UPSC, SSC, IBPS, RRB and other exam boards. However, always verify from the official exam notification before applying, as eligibility criteria can change slightly each year.
Who built GovtExamPath? ⌄
GovtExamPath was built by Sanjana Sarvani, a student from Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. She built this platform to help fellow Indian students navigate the confusing world of government exams — completely free, with no coaching promotion.
