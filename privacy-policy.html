👋 Introduction

Welcome to GovtExamPath ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit our website at govtexampath.netlify.app. Please read it carefully.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Usage Data: Pages you visit, time spent on pages, browser type, device type, and IP address — collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to improve your experience and help us understand how our site is used.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us, we may collect your name and email address.

✅ We do NOT collect sensitive personal information like passwords, financial data, or government ID numbers.

🔧 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve and optimize our website content and user experience
  • Understand which exam topics and career guidance content is most helpful
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Monitor website traffic and performance

🤝 Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google Analytics — tracks website usage and visitor behavior
  • Google AdSense — displays ads and uses cookies for ad personalization
  • Netlify — hosts our website and may log server access data

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them on their respective websites.

🍪 Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Analytics cookies — to understand how visitors use our site
  • Advertising cookies — placed by Google AdSense to show relevant ads

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though some features of the site may not function properly.

🔒 Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

👶 Children's Privacy

GovtExamPath is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

✏️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by using browser extensions like uBlock Origin
  • Opt out of personalized ads via Google Ad Settings

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Questions about your privacy?
