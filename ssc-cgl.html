📋 About SSC CGL

Staff Selection Commission – Combined Graduate Level

  • Conducted by Staff Selection Commission (SSC) every year
  • Recruits for Group B and C posts in Central Government
  • Top posts: Income Tax Inspector, CBI Sub-Inspector, Auditor, Accountant, ASO
  • Minimum qualification: Any graduation from a recognized university
  • One of the largest central govt recruitment exams — 11+ lakh applicants

📝 Exam Pattern (4 Tiers)

SSC CGL 2024-25 follows a 4-tier selection process

Tier 1 — Objective (Online)

  • General Intelligence & Reasoning — 25 Qs, 50 marks
  • General Awareness — 25 Qs, 50 marks
  • Quantitative Aptitude — 25 Qs, 50 marks
  • English Comprehension — 25 Qs, 50 marks
  • Total: 100 Qs, 200 marks, 60 minutes
  • Negative marking: -0.5 per wrong answer

Tier 2 — Objective + Descriptive (Online)

  • Paper 1: Math & Reasoning — 300 marks, 3 hrs
  • Paper 2: English — 200 marks, 2 hrs (for JSO/AAO)
  • Paper 3: Statistics — 200 marks, 2 hrs (for JSO)
  • Descriptive: Essay + Letter writing — 100 marks, 1 hr
  • Computer Skill Test (Mandatory for certain posts)

⏱️ Recommended Study Plan

  • 6 months minimum for a fresh start
  • 4 hrs/day: 2 hrs subject + 1 hr PYQ + 1 hr revision
  • Start mock tests from Month 3 onwards
  • Last 2 months: Only mocks + weak area focus
  • Target: 3+ full mock tests per week before exam
