🎯 GATE Preparation Tips

1
Score Full Marks in GA
General Aptitude is 15 marks — easiest section. Most students ignore it. Practice verbal reasoning and basic maths daily for 20 minutes.
2
Use Virtual Calculator Daily
GATE gives a virtual calculator, not physical. Practice calculations on it daily. Many students lose time here in the actual exam.
3
Don't Skip Engg Maths
13 marks from Engg Maths — most students skip this. It's the easiest 13 marks you'll ever get. Linear Algebra and Probability are most important.
4
Apply to Multiple PSUs
Your GATE score is valid for 3 years. Apply to ONGC, BHEL, NTPC, IOCL all at once. Each has different cutoffs — apply to all!
