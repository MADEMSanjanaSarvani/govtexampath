🏛️ TSPSC Exams Overview

Telangana State Public Service Commission — headquartered in Hyderabad

🥇 TSPSC Group 1

  • Deputy Collector, DSP, ACP, District Registrar
  • Qualification: Any Graduation
  • Age: 18–44 years (General) — higher limit vs many states
  • Salary: ₹56,100–₹1,77,500/month
  • Prelims → Mains (8 papers) → Interview
  • Strong focus on Telangana history, culture, movement

🥈 TSPSC Group 2

  • Junior Assistant, Revenue Inspector, Sub-Registrar
  • Qualification: Any Graduation
  • Age: 18–44 years
  • Salary: ₹28,940–₹83,140/month
  • Written Exam (no interview)
  • ~900–1500 vacancies per cycle

🥉 TSPSC Group 4

  • Junior Steno, Junior Assistant (clerical posts)
  • Qualification: Graduation / 12th (depends on post)
  • Age: 18–44 years
  • Salary: ₹19,000–₹56,100/month
  • Largest exam by vacancies — 5,000+ seats
  • Typing test required for steno/assistant posts

⚙️ TSPSC AEE

  • Assistant Executive Engineer — Engineering posts
  • Qualification: BE/BTech in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, ECE
  • Age: 18–44 years
  • Salary: ₹56,100–₹1,77,500/month
  • One of the best-paying TS state posts for engineers

🏫 TS TET + DSC (Teachers)

  • Telangana Teacher Eligibility Test + District Selection
  • SGT (Primary), SA (Secondary), PGT (Degree College)
  • Qualification: B.Ed + relevant subject degree
  • Salary: ₹28,940–₹1,04,340/month
  • TS TET clearing is mandatory for DSC application

📋 Panchayat Secretary

  • Village Revenue Officer / Panchayat Secretary
  • Qualification: Graduation
  • Age: 18–44 years
  • Salary: ₹19,000–₹38,000/month
  • Good entry-level government job with stability
  • ~6,000+ vacancies in last cycle (2022)

📝 TSPSC Group 1 Exam Pattern

Prelims — General Studies & Mental Ability

  • 150 questions — 150 marks — 2.5 hours
  • Indian History, Polity, Economy, Science
  • Telangana history & movement — heavy weightage
  • Current Affairs — National & Telangana
  • Objective type — qualifying for Mains

Mains — 8 Papers (1200 marks)

  • Paper 1: General Essay — 150 marks
  • Paper 2: History, Tradition & Culture — 150 marks
  • Paper 3: Indian Polity & AP/TS Acts — 150 marks
  • Paper 4: Economy & Development — 150 marks
  • Paper 5: Science, Technology, Environment — 150 marks
  • Papers 6-8: Telangana Specific subjects — 150 marks each

  • Telangana Movement — Separate statehood history (mandatory)
  • Telangana Geography — Rivers, Reservoirs, Godavari basin
  • TS Economy — Budget, Schemes like KCR Kit, Rythu Bandhu
  • Famous personalities from Telangana
  • TSRTC, HMDA, Hyderabad Metropolitan Area

🏆 Success Tips for TSPSC

  • Telangana-specific topics carry 30-40% marks — do NOT skip
  • Read V6, TV9 Telugu news daily for TS current affairs
  • Previous papers (2015-2024) are your best study material
  • Age limit of 44 is a big advantage — more time to prepare
  • No interview for Group 2 and 4 — written exam score is final
