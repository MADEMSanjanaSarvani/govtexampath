What is TSPSC?
Your gateway to Telangana state government jobs

The Telangana State Public Service Commission (TSPSC) is the state-level body that recruits candidates for various government positions in Telangana. If you're from anywhere in Telangana, TSPSC exams are your most direct route to a stable, well-paying government job in your home state.


TSPSC conducts multiple exams throughout the year — from Group 1 (the most prestigious, equivalent to state IAS) to Group 4 (entry-level clerical positions). Unlike UPSC or SSC, TSPSC exams have a strong focus on Telangana history, culture, economy and current affairs — giving local students a significant advantage.


The exams are conducted in Telugu and English, and reservation benefits apply as per Telangana state rules (OBC, SC, ST, EWS).

📋 Group 1 Selection Process

1
Preliminary Exam
150 MCQ questions, 150 marks, 2.5 hours. General Studies only. This is qualifying — just pass it.
2
Mains Exam
6 papers, descriptive answers. Telangana history & current affairs is heavily tested. 1500 total marks.
3
Interview
75 marks interview. Covers your personality, awareness of Telangana issues and administrative thinking.
4
Final Merit List
Based on Mains + Interview marks. Post allotment based on rank and your preference form.

📋 Group 2 & Group 4 Process

1
Group 2: Prelims
100 MCQ questions, 100 marks. General Studies + Telangana focus. Qualifying round.
2
Group 2: Mains
3 papers, descriptive. Paper I: Essay. Paper II: General Studies. Paper III: Telangana History & Society.
3
Group 4: Single Paper
200 MCQ questions, 200 marks, 2.5 hours. No interview. Direct selection on merit.
4
Document Verification
All exams end with DV. Keep all originals ready — caste certificate, degree, residence proof.
🎯 TSPSC Preparation Strategy
Proven tips from TSPSC toppers and coaching experts
📜
Master Telangana History First
Start with Telangana history — Kakatiyas, Nizams, Hyderabad state merger, Telangana movement. This gives you 15–20% marks that others miss. Read the Telangana state textbooks (free PDF available).
📰
Follow Eenadu / Sakshi Daily
Read Eenadu or Sakshi newspaper daily for Telangana current affairs. 10–15 questions in every TSPSC exam come from state-level news. Takes only 30 minutes a day.
📚
NCERT + TS State Board Books
For Group 1 & 2: NCERT 6–12 for GK foundation, then TS State Board books for Telangana-specific content. These are freely available on the Telangana government website.
✍️
Practice Telugu Answer Writing
Group 1 Mains is descriptive in Telugu or English. Practice writing 200-word answers daily from Month 3 onwards. Clarity and structure matter more than length.
🎯
Take TS-Specific Mock Tests
Use Manabadi, Sakshi Education, or TSPSC Academy mock tests — they are specifically designed for TSPSC pattern with Telangana questions. At least 2 mocks per week.
🏛️
Know All Govt Schemes
Memorize all Telangana government schemes: Rythu Bandhu, Rythu Bima, KCR Kit, Mission Bhagiratha, Kaleshwaram Project, Dharani portal. These come every year!
