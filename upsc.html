🏛️ Exam Overview

What is UPSC CSE?

The Civil Services Examination by UPSC is India's most prestigious exam. It selects officers for IAS, IPS, IFS and other All India Services and Central Services.


PrelimsMainsInterviewSalary: ₹56,100–₹2,50,000Age: 21–32 years
🥇 Top Services
  • Indian Administrative Service (IAS)
  • Indian Police Service (IPS)
  • Indian Foreign Service (IFS)
  • Indian Revenue Service (IRS)
  • Indian Audit & Accounts Service (IAAS)
📚 Syllabus Overview

Prelims – GS Paper I

Current Events, Indian History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Environment & Ecology, Science

Prelims – CSAT

Comprehension, Logical Reasoning, Analytical Ability, Basic Numeracy, Data Interpretation

Mains – Essay

Two essays on socio-economic, political, philosophical and abstract topics. 250 marks total.

Mains – GS Papers I–IV

History, Geography, Governance, Polity, Economy, Social Justice, Ethics & Integrity

Mains – Optional

Choose any 1 subject from 48 options including History, Geography, Sociology, PSIR, Anthropology etc.

Personality Test

275 marks interview to assess suitability for civil services. Tests mental alertness, leadership and integrity.

🎯 Preparation Strategy
1

Read NCERT Books Class 6–12 First

Cover all subjects — History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Science. This is the most important step.

2

Read Standard Reference Books

Laxmikanth for Polity, Bipin Chandra for History, Ramesh Singh for Economy, GC Leong for Geography.

3

Read The Hindu Daily

Spend 1–2 hours daily. Focus on editorials, governance issues and international affairs.

4

Choose Optional Subject Early

Pick your optional based on your graduation background and interest. Start preparation in year 1.

5

Answer Writing Practice

Practice writing 150 and 250 word answers daily from year 1. This is critical for Mains success.
